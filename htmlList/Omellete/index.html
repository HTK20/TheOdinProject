How make an Omellete?

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Eggs
  • 2 Tablespoon of Onion
  • 1 Tablespoon of Butter
  • Salt (As per taste)

Instructions

  1. Crack those eggs in a bowl
  2. Beat eggs throughly untill bubble start forming around edges
  3. Add onions to the mix
  4. Add salt as per taste
  5. Heat the pan on medium flame
  6. Add butter to the pan
  7. Pour the mix and cook it for 2 minutes
  8. Flip the Omellete and cook the other side as well
  9. Voila! Your yummy Omellete is ready.
